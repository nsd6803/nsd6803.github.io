Company

Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE) is the world leader in interactive entertainment software. The company represents the PlayStation® brand and family of products and services. PlayStation has been an innovator in the market since the launch of the first PlayStation in Japan in 1994. The PlayStation family of products and services includes PlayStation®5, PlayStation®4, PlayStation®VR, PlayStation™Store, PlayStation®Plus and PlayStation software from PlayStation Studios. The company is headquartered in San Mateo, California. SIE is a subsidiary of Sony Group Corporation and performs global functions. It is headquartered in California, London and Tokyo.
